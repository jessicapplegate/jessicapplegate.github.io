SHAPE JOY

A developmental psychopathology study to extend the period which empathic joy is experienced by children.

Project Overview

A virtual research study that employed the use of surveys, behavioral tasks, observational methods, and thematic analysis.

As a research assistant, I wore many hats:

  • Recruited participants and scheduled initial pre-assessments.
  • Tracked recruitment using Excel.
  • Recorded observations during behavioral tasks with participants.
  • Modified research protocols with WebEx, updated behavioral tasks used during sessions, and updated Qualtrics surveys.
