Age-Friendly University Study

A funded research project by the Gerontology Insitute to evaluate age-friendly supports and barriers for life-long learners on GSU's campus in Atlanta, GA.

Project Overview

Participatory research study that employed the use of focus groups, interview, and survey.

As a research lead, I did the following:

  • Designed study protocols, iterated based on IRB feedback, and designed recruitement materials.
  • Tracked project progress using SharePoint.
  • Performed extensive literature review to design resaerch protocols and determine methods.
  • Presented project to Gerontology Insitute for funding.
  • Study is on-going at Georgia State University thanks to foundation laid by myself and my mentor, Dr. Barber.

Additional Details

  • Tools: Sharepoint, Canva, Qualtrics.
  • Methods: Focus Group, Interview, Survey, Literature Review.
  • Results: Project is in progress, completion by the end of Spring 2025.
