Overview

At Workiva, I work within a high-stakes enterprise environment, conducting research to simplify complex financial reporting and compliance workflows. My work focuses on bridging the gap between sophisticated technical requirements and intuitive user experiences for accounting and finance professionals. Currently, a lot of my work is on automation and how agentic AI can be leveraged in regulated contexts.

The Challenge

Designing for enterprise means managing high-stakes data and collaborative complexity. I bridge the gap between legacy mental models and modern efficiency by uncovering friction in expert environments. By thoughtfully integrating AI into existing 'pro-tool' workflows, I enable users to meet strict regulatory demands with greater speed and fewer errors.

My Role

While many of my specific findings are protected, these highlights demonstrate the methods and technical rigor I apply to my work:

  • Concept & Usability Testing: Executes iterative study designs to validate agentic AI workflows, ensuring high-stakes tools remain intuitive for finance and accounting experts.
  • Information Architecture Audits: Streamlined navigation structures within the platform to minimize cognitive load during high-pressure reporting cycles.
  • Qualitative Data Analysis & Coding: Synthesizes complex interview data through thematic coding to transform raw user feedback into actionable product requirements.
  • In-Depth User Interviews: Engaging with specialized subject matter experts (SMEs) to uncover latent needs and mental models within regulated, professional environments.
  • Quantitative Data Analysis: Leverages R to analyze feature adoption and complex user behavior patterns.
